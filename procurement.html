Log Procurement

We purchase birch roundwood.

Volume and diameter group measurement:
Diameter groups for veneer logs are determined based on the measured end diameter under bark using the TIMBETER photo processing technology in accordance with LVS 82:2003.

Hand meansurement according to under bark diameter can be used. For TARA coeficent measurement is applied.

Birch logs

Payable lengths:
- 2.70 m (delivered length: 2.80 m)
- 3.20 m (delivered length: 3.30 m)
- 3.30 m (delivered length: 3.40 m)

Log measurement time: every working day from 08:00 to 18:00
