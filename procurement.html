Log Procurement

We purchase birch roundwood.

Volume and diameter group measurement:
Hand meansurement according to under bark diameter as well as coefficient measurement is applied.

Payable lengths:
- 2.70 m (delivered length: 2.80 m)
- 3.00 m (delivered length: 3.10 m)
- 3.20 m (delivered length: 3.30 m)
- 3.30 m (delivered length: 3.40 m)

Log measurement time: every working day from 07:00 to 19:00

Requirements for Logs

  • Curvature per meter no more than 2 cm (within the cut)
  • S-shaped curvature is not allowed
  • Minimal branching (no more than three branches per meter)
  • Freshly sawn (staining logs are not suitable for production)
  • Maximum diameter 40cm (if exceeded, then will be marked as defective)
