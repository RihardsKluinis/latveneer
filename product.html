Production Process

From raw logs to sorted veneer

1

Logs are peeled on two spindleless lines, at natural moisture levels without presoaking. Thickness and quality are monitored regularly.

2

Fresh veneer rests for at least 48 hours, allowing natural redistribution of moisture.

3

A 32m, four-belt dryer powered by recycled woodchips brings veneer to the required moisture levels

4

Hourly inspections ensure the ideal 4-8% moisture range is maintained.

5

Each sheet is manually inspected and sorted into grade packs for consistent quality.
